My Life Thusfar...

The Early Days

In the beginning, the Earth's crust cooled, there was a boy, born in Hanoi, in a happy little family, with 2 sisters.

The boy was very happy, and he was grateful that his family was a happy one. But it was still early, and life has so much more for him.

The boys have a few commitments and responsibilities that he must embrace:

  • He is the future family head, and it was impressed upon him that he has ties to his hometown.
  • He is the son of two engineers, and he must lived up to their expectation of achivements.

Therefore, his childhood, though mostly happy, was not unrestrained. He must commit himself to discipline and commitment:

  1. Studying comes first.
  2. Only hang out with friends his parents will approve.

He has many interests, as boys of his age typically do. But some of them were suppressed, to make ways for interests which his father believed to be more beneficial to his development.
